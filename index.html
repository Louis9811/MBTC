Who We Are

Bridging the
Skills Gap

Mufuka Business and Technical College (MBTC) is a leading TVET provider in the Hammanskraal region, based at the Skampaneng Skills Centre. We empower matriculants, unemployed youth, and working professionals with the technical expertise needed to thrive in today's dynamic job market.
